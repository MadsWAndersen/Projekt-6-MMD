    Inputs are used quite often throughout the product. Both in the shape of checkboxes and text inputs. Both the styling and the size of these inputs varies from page to page.

    A lot of the inputs used in the product fit on one page but not the next. Therefore, we've added some diffent examples of the many different types of inputs we've used.

    Some have been made with a both a different background color as well as different overall colors, than others. They have also been listed as examples:

    IKONER SKAL RETTES

    Icons need to be used the right way. The icons in this product have therefore been used because there is meaning behind them. They add to the overall style of the products and keep the pages from turning into nothing but boxes with text. Icons help make the pages more dynamic.

    We have used: FONT AWESOME. It's free and it has a wide library of icons to choose from.
